## H1: Hassle

*What processes in your daily life are tedious, error-prone, or needlessly manual?*

**WHO has this problem?**
Solo indie game developers or student UI/UX designers in Georgia who are "technical" but lack professional illustration
skills. Specifically, those who need high-quality, scalable vector assets (icons, UI elements, or character sprites) for
their prototypes.

**PROBLEM in one sentence:**
Moving from a creative "vibe" or a rough hand-drawn sketch to a clean, usable `.svg` or high-res `.png` file requires
hours of manual "pen-tool" work in software like Figma or Illustrator.

**CURRENT SOLUTION:**
They search for "free" asset packs on sites like itch.io or Flaticon, which rarely match their specific art style, or
they attempt to draw them manually, resulting in "programmer art" that looks unprofessional.

**AI ANGLE:**
**Multi-agent reasoning and Vision-to-Code generation.** A "Creative Agent" (LLM) interprets the aesthetic details and
technical constraints of a user's prompt or sketch. A second "Technical Agent" then uses **Image-to-Image generation**
to create a reference and converts it into **SVG XML code** to produce a mathematically clean, scalable vector file.

**WHY IT MATTERS:**
It reduces the "Concept-to-Asset" time from hours to seconds. It allows developers to maintain a consistent visual style
across an entire project without the high cost of hiring an external artist, enabling faster iteration and
higher-quality MVP releases.

## H2: Hardship

*What problem do you see in your community or country that affects real people?*

**WHO has this problem?**
Small-scale artisanal producers in regional Georgia (e.g., Tushetian knitters, Kakhetian woodworkers, or carpet weavers)
who want to modernize their traditional patterns for international digital markets.

**PROBLEM in one sentence:**
Traditional Georgian motifs found on physical heritage items are difficult to digitize into the precise vector formats
required for modern production tools like CNC wood cutters or digital embroidery machines.

**CURRENT SOLUTION:**
Artisans either stick to slow, purely manual production, or they pay expensive design agencies in Tbilisi to manually
trace photos of physical work into digital vectors, which is often cost-prohibitive for a village-based business.

**AI ANGLE:**
**Multimodal Visual Reasoning and Asset Extraction.** The system uses a vision-language model to analyze a
high-resolution photo of a physical object (like a hand-woven rug). It identifies repeating geometric motifs and uses *
*image-to-vector segmentation** to isolate the traditional pattern, automatically cleaning up "noise" from the fabric
texture to create a production-ready digital blueprint.

**WHY IT MATTERS:**
This provides economic empowerment for rural communities. It allows traditional Georgian heritage to be scaled onto
modern products (like laser-cut jewelry or modern apparel), helping micro-businesses compete globally while preserving
their cultural identity.

## H3: Horizon

*What new capability does multimodal AI unlock that was impossible 2 years ago?*

**WHO has this problem?**
Blind or low-vision university students in Georgia who need to interpret complex visual data like flowcharts, organic
chemistry diagrams, or Georgian-language maps in their science and engineering textbooks.

**PROBLEM in one sentence:**
Standard screen readers can read text, but they cannot explain the **spatial relationships** in a diagram (e.g., "The '
Start' box is connected to three different 'Process' boxes"), leaving these students excluded from high-level STEM
fields.

**CURRENT SOLUTION:**
Relying on a sighted peer or professor to sit with them and describe the image, or using expensive, rare tactile (
braille-embossed) graphics that are difficult to produce for every single textbook page.

**AI ANGLE:**
**Spatial Reasoning and Visual Question Answering (VQA).** Unlike older AI that provided static captions, a multimodal
agent can "reason" about the layout. A student can ask, "Where does the arrow from the top-left box point?" and the AI
uses **coordinate-aware vision** to explain the logical flow of the diagram in real-time.

**WHY IT MATTERS:**
This represents true educational autonomy. This technology turns every static image in a textbook into an interactive,
conversational tutor, removing a massive structural barrier for visually impaired students who want to study
engineering, medicine, or data science.
